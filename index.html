Doors of drums

Context of the project

The goal is to create a video game focused on discovering different percussion instruments with which the player will be able to interact, which will seek to motivate children to learn more about the world of percussion. It will work hand in hand with the Percussive Arts Society, which is the world's largest percussion organization and the central source of information and networking for percussionists of all ages.

Objectives of the project

Teaching the basic principles (theory) of percussion.

Discovery of different percussion instruments according to level of complexity for execution and recommended age to play.

To inspire future generations to enter the world of percussion.
