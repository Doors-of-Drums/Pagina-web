Repeating sounds

The player will be presented with sounds that will repeat every so often in each level. The player must press the keys, which will sound certain sounds, in the correct order to open the different doors. The keys that can be pressed depend on the level of difficulty in which you are playing.

Walk

The player has the opportunity to explore the different rooms in order to discover which door is the right one.

Rotate

The player will be able to rotate on his axis allowing him to see all his surroundings and walk freely around the available space in the room.
